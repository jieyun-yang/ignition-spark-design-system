# Brand Identity

## Mission

To transform the way professional service providers and their clients do business together.

## Brand Pillars

| Pillar | Description |
|:-------|:------------|
| **Clarity** | Ignition gives customers and clients total clarity on the engagement, making doing business together simple. |
| **Confidence** | At every touchpoint, Ignition gives customers and their clients confidence about doing business together. |
| **Control** | Ignition automates and streamlines tasks, so customers can focus on what matters most - their clients. |
| **Convenience** | Ignition plays with business critical apps to allow customers to run their business on autopilot. |

## Application

The brand mission and pillars serve as a compass for all activities:
- Design decisions
- Marketing messages
- Feature development
- Customer interactions

Every touchpoint should reflect commitment to the mission and embody Clarity, Confidence, Control, and Convenience.

## Best Practices

### Do's
- **Align with Mission:** Ensure initiatives, product changes, and communications align with transforming how professional service providers do business
- **Embody the Pillars:** Integrate Clarity, Confidence, Control, and Convenience into all design solutions, user flows, content, and interactions
- **Consistent Messaging:** Use the language and spirit of brand pillars to maintain consistent voice across touchpoints

### Don'ts
- Don't deviate from brand pillars without clear justification
- Don't sacrifice clarity for cleverness
- Don't create confusion about what Ignition stands for

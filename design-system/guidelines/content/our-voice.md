# Our Voice

## Overview

This guideline defines the fundamental principles of our brand's voice and personality, ensuring consistent and effective communication across all platforms.

## Voice Pillars

### Straight Up
Communicate clearly and directly, without unnecessary jargon or overly complex phrasing. Be honest and transparent.

### Charismatic
Infuse warmth, energy, and a positive outlook. Be engaging and approachable, making interactions feel personable and inviting.

### Resourceful
Provide helpful, practical, and solution-oriented information. Empower users with knowledge and guidance that supports their goals.

## Language Principles

- **Clarity:** Always prioritize clear and unambiguous language
- **Consistency:** Maintain consistent terminology and phrasing
- **Audience Focus:** Tailor language appropriately for context

## Language & Regionalization

Adapt to regional language differences for cultural appropriateness:

| Concept | AU/UK/NZ | US/CA | Approach |
|:--------|:---------|:------|:---------|
| Financial year | Financial year | Fiscal year | Regionalize |
| Postcode | Postcode | ZIP code (US), Postal code (CA) | Regionalize |
| Cheque | Cheque | Check | Regionalize |
| Tick | Tick | Check | Use "Check" |
| Fortnightly | Fortnightly | Biweekly | Use "Every 2 weeks" |
| Expiry | Expiry | Expiration | Use "Expiration" |
